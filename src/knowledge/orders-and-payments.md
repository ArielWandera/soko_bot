# Orders and Payments on Soko

## How the order flow works
1. Buyer adds to cart and pays at checkout via Mobile Money (MTN or Airtel)
2. Payment is processed through PesaPal and held in escrow
3. Farmer receives a notification and confirms or rejects within 24 hours
4. If confirmed: farmer dispatches, marks as In Transit, then Delivered
5. On completion: payment is released to the farmer

## Payment methods
- **MTN Mobile Money**
- **Airtel Money**

Both are processed through PesaPal. You will receive a payment prompt on your phone at checkout. Soko does not store your PIN or Mobile Money credentials.

## What happens if I pay but the farmer rejects my order?
You get a full refund back to your Mobile Money account. Refunds are typically processed within 24–48 hours depending on your network.

## What happens if payment fails?
Your cart is saved. Check your Mobile Money balance and retry from the cart page. If you were charged but the order didn't go through, contact support — every PesaPal transaction is logged with a tracking ID.

## Can I cancel an order after placing it?
You can cancel an order that is still in **Placed** status (before the farmer confirms). Once a farmer confirms, cancellation is not guaranteed — message the farmer directly.

## Order notification flow
- **Buyer** gets notified when: farmer confirms, order is dispatched, order is delivered
- **Farmer** gets notified when: new order is placed, buyer messages them

Notifications arrive in-app and via the Soko notifications page (/user/notifications).

## Farmer payout timing
Payments are released to the farmer once an order reaches **Delivered** status. Payouts go to the Mobile Money number registered on the farmer's profile.

## Disputes
A dispute can be raised within 48 hours of delivery if:
- You received the wrong produce
- Quantity was significantly short
- Produce was in unacceptable condition

Raise a dispute from your order detail page. Soko reviews the case and issues a refund if valid. Repeated valid disputes against a farmer affect their account standing.

## Common issues and fixes

**"My payment prompt didn't come through"**
Wait 2 minutes and check your Mobile Money inbox. If nothing, retry payment from the cart.

**"I paid but my order still shows as Placed"**
The farmer has up to 24 hours to confirm. You'll get a notification when they do.

**"The farmer confirmed but hasn't dispatched"**
Message the farmer directly from the order page. If no response within a reasonable time, raise a dispute.

**"I was charged twice"**
Contact Soko support with your PesaPal transaction ID (found in your Mobile Money statement). Duplicate charges are rare but are refunded promptly.
