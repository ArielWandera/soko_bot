# Soko Buyer Guide

## Browsing the marketplace
You don't need an account to browse. Go to /marketplace to see all active listings. Filter by:
- **Category** — vegetables, grains, fruits, cash_crops, dairy, herbs, livestock
- **District** — find farmers near you to reduce transport costs
- **Price range** — set a minimum and maximum price per unit

## Finding specific produce
Use the search bar to find a crop by name (e.g. "tomatoes", "maize", "matoke"). Results show price per unit, available quantity, farmer name, and district.

## Placing an order
1. Open a listing to see full details, farmer profile, and reviews
2. Set your quantity (must meet the farmer's minimum order)
3. Add to cart
4. Go to checkout and confirm your delivery details
5. Pay via MTN or Airtel Money — you'll get a payment prompt on your phone

Your order is **placed** once payment goes through. The farmer then has 24 hours to confirm.

## Order statuses
- **Placed** — payment received, waiting for farmer to confirm
- **Confirmed** — farmer accepted, preparing your order
- **In Transit** — farmer has dispatched
- **Delivered** — order complete
- **Cancelled** — cancelled before dispatch (full refund)
- **Rejected** — farmer couldn't fulfill (full refund)

Track your orders at /profile.

## Payments
Soko uses PesaPal to process MTN Mobile Money and Airtel Money payments. After you confirm your order at checkout, you'll receive a prompt on your phone to enter your PIN. Do not close the app until the payment confirms.

If payment fails: check your Mobile Money balance, then retry from the cart. Your cart is saved.

If you were charged but the order didn't go through, contact Soko support — PesaPal transactions are logged and refunds are processed within 24–48 hours.

## Getting recommendations
Once you've placed a few orders, Soko's recommendation engine suggests farmers based on:
- What you've ordered before
- Farmers in your district (lower transport cost)
- Farmer ratings and fulfillment rates

Ask SokoBot "what should I buy?" or "recommend something for me" to see your personalised list.

## Reviewing a farmer
After an order is completed, you can leave a star rating (1–5) and a comment. Reviews help other buyers and directly affect which farmers get recommended. Be honest and specific.

## Messaging a farmer
Before or after ordering, you can message a farmer directly from their listing page. Use this to ask about stock availability, harvest dates, or delivery arrangements. Go to /messages to see all conversations.

## Returning or disputing an order
If you receive wrong produce or significantly less than ordered, report it from your order detail page within 48 hours of delivery. Soko reviews disputes and processes refunds where appropriate.

## Buying without a smartphone
USSD access is available for checking prices. Full ordering requires the Soko app or website.
